Mihalik-Thompson Stadium Complex

Like to work out or play sports? Visit the ARC! The 82,000 square foot facility has components for everyone! Including an aquatic center, rock climbing wall, and a Fitness center!

Outdoor Activities

Menu

Living Treasures Wild Animal Park

What’s better than visiting the zoo? Living Treasures is located in New Castle, PA, and with over 400 animals of 70 different species, there is a wonderful environment for everyone!

McConnells Mill State Park

Want to visit one of Pennsylvania’s must see state parks? Visit McConnells Mill! The scenic gorge, watermill, and covered bridge set in the middle of the wilderness makes this one of the most exciting places to visit in Pennsylvania.

Freedom Falls and Rockland Furnace

Freedom falls is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Western Pennsylvania. Don’t like the water? Visit the Rockland furnace or the Rockland Tunnel. There is something for everyone at Freedom Falls.

Old Thompson Field

Want a big wide open space to play with friends or to run around? Look no further than Old Thompson Field, which used to be where the football games were played until 1974.

Wally Rose Field

Not a fan of Old Thompson Field and want somewhere to go play with friends? Go to Wally Rose Field. Big open field to run around and do whatever with your friends!

SRU Alumni Pavilion

Want somewhere to go with your friends outside, but it’s raining? Go to the SRU Alumni Pavilion! Enjoy the great outdoors, but have something over your head so you don’t have to enjoy the rain or snow!

Shopping

Menu

Grove City Outlets

Want to go to the mall, but don’t want to travel far? Lucky for you, the Grove City outlets is located right along Interstate 79 in Grove City. With stores such as Starbucks, Nike, and American Eagle, there is something for everyone!

Aldi’s

All the students at SRU know the struggle of going to college and not being able to afford much. That’s why stores such as Aldi’s are so great! With insanely cheap groceries, Aldi’s in Grove City is a must for all who visit Slippery Rock.

Tinker’s Dam

Need flowers for that special man or lady in your life? Lucky for you, there’s a great little flower shop located right in Slippery Rock! Whether you need a couple flowers or a large number of flowers for an event, Tinker’s Dam will give you what you need.

Nearby Places of Interest

Menu

Old Stone House

Like history, or viewing how people lived years ago? Look no further than the Old Stone House. Located right down the street from SRU, the historic landmark gives visitors a glimpse into the past, at what life used to be like before smart phones and Juuls….

Moraine State Park

Like being near the water? How about taking a visit down to Moraine State park? With a 3,225-acre lake, there is no shortage of water for visitors. Also, the large number of trails makes sure that everyone in the family has something to do.

Iron Mountain

Inside the little town of Boyers, PA lives a little secret…. Known as Iron Mountain. Tons of classified information is stored beneath the Earth in a highly secured environment. Celebrities such as Ozzy Osbourne and Slash have been known to be seen down in Iron Mountain. Stop by, you never know what you might see!
